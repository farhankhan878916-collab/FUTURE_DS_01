    📌 Key Insights

    1. GrowthRevenue grew 8.97% year-over-year (2024 → 2025), with consistent seasonal spikes every Oct–Dec (festive/holiday buying) roughly 60% above the January–February baseline.
    2. ConcentrationTechnology drives 58% of revenue but carries the thinnest margin (14%), while Office Supplies is only 1.5% of revenue but the highest margin (32%).
    3. ProductThe Laptop alone generates ~27% of total company revenue — a single-SKU dependency risk.
    4. RegionNorth and East together contribute 55% of sales; West underperforms by ~₹3.5L monthly vs. North despite similar order volume.
    5. Fulfilment60% of orders ship Standard — a potential upsell lever, since Express/Same-Day orders show comparable basket sizes.

    🎯 Recommendations

    • Diversify category mix: Actively cross-sell Office Supplies & Home Appliances (high margin) alongside Technology purchases to lift blended profitability.
    • Protect the holiday surge: Start inventory build-up and marketing spend in September to fully capture the Oct–Dec demand spike; extend similar promos into slow Jan–Feb months.
    • De-risk Laptop dependency: Bundle Laptops with Monitors/Mouse/Bags to raise attach-rate revenue instead of relying on single-unit sales.
    • Invest in West region: Audit pricing, local marketing and delivery speed — West has the same order count trend as South but a measurable revenue gap.
    • Promote faster shipping: Test a small Express-shipping fee waiver for orders above the average order value to shift the mix and improve customer experience.
